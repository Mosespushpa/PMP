# Product Overview

A full-featured personal music player web application for playing Christian worship songs. The app provides a complete music listening experience with playlist management, favorites, and standard music player controls. The interface is designed for easy browsing, organizing, and playback of a curated collection of religious music.

## Key Features

- Visual grid layout of songs with album artwork
- Standard music player controls (play, pause, next, previous, seek, volume)
- Auto-play next song when current track ends
- Playlist creation and management
- Favorite songs marking and filtering
- Now playing display with track information
- Queue management
- Persistent user preferences (favorites, playlists saved locally)
